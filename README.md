# Amazon-E-Commerce-Sales-Performance-Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze Amazon e-commerce sales performance.
The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful insights to help understand **sales trends, customer behavior, regional performance, and product sales patterns**.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate practical **data analytics, data modeling, and business intelligence visualization skills** using Power BI.

## 🎯 Business Problem

E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of sales data. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify trends, evaluate product performance, or measure business growth.

This dashboard helps answer key business questions such as:

* How are sales performing over time?
* Which regions generate the highest revenue?
* What is the average order value?
* Are current sales meeting business targets?
* How many customers and products contribute to overall sales?

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset used in this project contains simulated Amazon e-commerce transaction data including:

* Orders
* Customers
* Products
* Product categories
* Regions
* Sales transactions

The dataset includes **9,994 records** representing **5,009 orders** across multiple regions and product categories.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights important business metrics:

* **Total Sales:** 2.30M
* **Total Orders:** 5,009
* **Total Products:** 1,351
* **Total Customers:** 793
* **Average Order Value (AOV):** 458.61
* **Current Month Sales:** 83.83K
* **Target Sales:** 106.70K

These KPIs provide a quick overview of business performance.

---

### 2️⃣ Sales Trend Analysis

The dashboard includes a **time-series visualization** showing sales by:

* Year
* Quarter
* Month

This helps identify **sales growth trends and seasonal patterns**.

---

### 3️⃣ Target vs Sales Performance

A comparison between **actual sales and target sales** allows evaluation of business performance.

For example:

* Current Month Sales: **83.83K**
* Target Sales: **106.70K**
* Performance Gap: **-21.43%**

This highlights areas where business targets are not being met.

---

### 4️⃣ Regional Sales Analysis

The dashboard allows users to explore **sales performance by region**, helping identify high-performing markets.

Users can compare regional contributions to total revenue.

---

### 5️⃣ Interactive Filters

The dashboard includes slicers that allow dynamic analysis:

* Product Category
* Sub-Category
* Region
* Sales Segment

These filters enable users to **drill down into specific segments of the data**.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

Some important insights from the analysis include:

Target Gap: Current month sales target se 21.43% peeche hain. Iske liye Regional Managers ke saath review meeting zaroori hai.

High Value: Average Order Value ($458.61) kaafi strong hai, jo high-end product sales ko indicate karta hai.

Scaling: 2014-2017 ka trend positive hai, yani business consistently expand ho raha hai.**.

These insights can help businesses optimize sales strategy and identify improvement opportunities.
